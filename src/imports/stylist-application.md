Design the Stylist Application Flow for ATELISTRY, a luxury personal style platform and professional network.

This page appears after a stylist selects “Apply for Access”.

The tone should feel editorial, respectful, and selective — not promotional or transactional.

This is an application, not a sign-up.

Visual Direction

Dark, restrained interface consistent with ATELISTRY

Elegant serif headlines

Clean sans-serif body text

Minimal form fields

Calm vertical rhythm

No bright highlights, no urgency cues

Page Header

Headline:
Apply for Access

Supporting line:
A professional network for personal stylists who practice with intention, depth, and clarity.

ATELISTRY is a curated platform designed to support meaningful, long-term stylist–client relationships.
Applications are reviewed to ensure alignment with our standards of practice, professionalism, and discretion.
