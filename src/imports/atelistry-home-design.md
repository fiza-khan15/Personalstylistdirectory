Design and refine the Home page for ATELISTRY, a luxury, editorial-style digital platform for personal stylists and image consultants.

The experience should feel like a modern fashion house meets a private professional society — minimal, restrained, confident, and timeless.

Do not position ATELISTRY as a directory or listing website.
It is a curated platform and evolving ecosystem with future capabilities including concierge hiring, long-term stylist relationships, and professional collaboration.

Visual Direction

Dark, cinematic background (charcoal black, near-black gradients)

High-contrast serif typography for headlines

Clean sans-serif for body text

Editorial spacing with generous negative space

Subtle deep-burgundy accents for dividers and CTAs

Minimal motion cues (fades, slow transitions)
